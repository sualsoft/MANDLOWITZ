In response to our employer clients and contacts observations that there is a general lack of training and education programming on workers’ compensation, MANDLOWITZ TRAINING INC. (MTI) has developed and is NOW offering a multi-module WSIB Education Program.

The intent of the WSIB Education Program is to provide a practical, evidence-based approach for the administration of worker accident claims in Ontario (WSIB). MTI believes that employers should be well positioned to proactively administer/manage their workplace in the event of an accident, injury or illness. The WSIB Education Program provides the tools for the establishment and/or enhancement of an organization’s WSIB program.

MTI’s WSIB Education Program consists of six (6) modules, each approximately two (2) hours in length.


We cover topics such as:

Your WSIB Policy/Procedure

Accident Investigation

Accident Reporting

Return to Work

Claims Management - assessing eligibility

Claims Management - objections and appeals
